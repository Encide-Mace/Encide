About Us

Encide Mace is one of the emerging enthusiastic Coding Clubs of Mar Athanasius College of Engineering Kothamangalam. Here students with different interests such as Competitive programming, App development, Web Development and Machine Learning gather up for collaborative learning. Being a Coding Club, we do pretty much everything and anything to enhance our member's coding skills. We frequently organize many events Workshops, Quizzes, Talk sections, Hackathons and contests are just to name a few.

Join us, where innovation meets collaboration! Dive into the world of programming, sharpen your coding skills, and build amazing projects with fellow enthusiasts. Let's code the future together!

Join our thriving community, and together, let's elevate our coding skills, compete on a global scale, and foster a coding culture that leaves a lasting impact on our society.
